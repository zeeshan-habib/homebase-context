# Homebase Product Launch Timeline

*Sources: Slack #celebration, #general, #business_reviews, #team-cashout, #p-and-p-core, #p-and-p-comms • Compiled February 2026*

---

## November 2015 — Stride
First named product launch celebrated company-wide. John Waldmann noted it was "a lot of work until the end to get it right."

---

## March 2016 — Revel Integration
Homebase went live with the Revel POS integration, completing sales training with 100 Revel reps.

---

## June 2018 — Hiring Product
Internal launch celebrated with a "Rodeo" themed costume contest. Makan was credited as the engineering lead, launching the product in his first year at Homebase.

---

## June 2020 — Health & Safety Checklist + Onboarding / Document Storage
Multi-product launch during the early COVID reopening period, alongside a full pricing page redesign and new packaging. Described internally as "a huge, ambitious, complex launch."

- **Health & Safety Checklist** — helped businesses and employees stay safe as they reopened
- **Onboarding & Document Storage** — enabled digital new-hire onboarding via email/Homebase
- **New pricing page** — new design, packaging, and messaging at joinhomebase.com/pricing

---

## July 2021 — Payroll + Cash Out (Public Announcement)
Official public launch of Homebase Payroll and Cash Out, coinciding with a fundraise announcement on "LWOM Day 4." John Waldmann noted: *"These products and the fundraise may be old news for us, but it's still a big deal."*

---

## February–March 2022 — Cash Out Instant Delivery Fee Increase
Price of instant Cash Out delivery raised from **$3.99 → $4.99**.

- Experiment launched ~Feb 10 at 10% of companies
- Rolled out to all users on **March 1, 2022**
- Rationale: underpriced vs. competitors; proceeds used to expand employee cash-out limits
- Result: no significant volume drop; best weekly Cash Out revenue ever ($70K+/week, ~$3.7M annualized)

---

## May–July 2022 — SaaS Plan Price Increase
Homebase's first broad subscription price increase, affecting Essentials and Plus plans. Rolled out in phases:

| Date | Change |
|------|--------|
| May 20 | Annual discount reduced from **30% → 20%** for new subscriptions and upgrades |
| May 23–25 | Customer comms sent; sales tax added in applicable states (effective May 25) |
| June 28 | New prices live for Homebase-billed (Braintree + Stripe) customers |
| July 31 | Clover-billed customers' price increase takes effect |

**New prices (effective June 28):**
- Essentials: **$24.95/mo**
- Plus: **$59.95/mo**

**Retention offer:** Grandfathered customers (on older pricing) received a **25% loyalty discount** on the new price. Final-save discounts for escalated customers: Essentials Monthly $14.50, Plus Monthly $37.50.

---

## September 2022 — Expenses Manager
Voted into production launch by the team.

---

## February 2023 — Talkdesk (Call Platform Migration)
Customer care teams migrated from Aircall to Talkdesk, enabling improved reporting, queue monitoring, and manager tools. Described as a "nearly flawless launch."

---

## May 2023 — Product Releases Page
Launched joinhomebase.com/product-releases/ to give customers a central hub for recent feature announcements.

---

## October 2023 — Bill Tracking
Rolled out to all Cash Out users. Helps hourly workers track upcoming bills, receive weekly alerts, and manage their total financial picture from within Homebase.

---

## November 2023 — Break Waivers
Launched on Plus & All-in-One plans. Allows managers to track and document employee breaks for compliance, particularly in high-regulation states like California and New York.

---

## December 2023 — Payroll Boost
New premium Payroll package introduced at $49/month + $9/employee/month, featuring:
- **Autopayroll** — runs payroll automatically every pay period with zero clicks
- **Next Day Pay** — employees paid out the morning after payroll is run

---

## February 2024 — Pay Any Day Card (General Availability)
Two years in the making. Rolled out the Pay Any Day (PAD) Card to general audience, allowing hourly workers to access earned wages before their regular payday. Described as "life-changing" by beta customers.

---

## April 2024 — $60M Series D Funding
Announced April 3, 2024. Coverage in TechCrunch, Fortune Term Sheet, Axios Pro Rata, and others. ~50M+ media impressions at announcement.

---

## April–May 2024 — Launch Week (Inaugural)
Largest integrated marketing campaign to date, timed to National Small Business Week (April 29). Key components:

- **Top Local Workplace Awards** — recognized 50K+ top U.S. local workplaces based on 1B+ data points
- **Homebase Releases** — new customer education motion spotlighting 10+ features shipped over the prior 6 months (inspired by Shopify Editions / Airbnb Releases)
- 650K+ lifecycle comms to customers and prospects
- 20+ in-product CommandBar campaigns

---

## June 2025 — Launch Week (AI on Main Street)
AI-focused launch campaign timed to National Small Business Week. Positioned as bringing AI tools to Main Street. Features included:

- **AI Hiring Assistant** — faster hiring with AI-driven applicant screening
- **AI Scheduling** — smarter schedules with reduced back-and-forth
- **Messaging** — new in-app communication tools
- **401(k)** — retirement plan offering
- **AI Chat** — conversational support for managers and employees
- New employee-facing financial features

---

## September 2025 — Pricing Migration (New Pricing Structure)
An ongoing migration of existing customers to a new pricing structure. As of September 9, 2025 (per WBR):

- **48%** of eligible customers moved to new pricing
- **36%** of Stripe-billed customers switched
- ~4.8K monthly customers still pending migration (targeting 52–53% total)
- Max attainable for the month estimated at **65–66%** of eligible locations
- 3–4K locations showing data discrepancies in reporting; investigation ongoing for mobile-billed customers

---

## September 2025 — Clover Embedded + Hiring Product Beta
- **Clover Embedded Pilot 2** — 5,000 locations live with basic functionality; GA target September 23, 2025; 15,000 additional locations planned for the following week
- **Hiring Product Beta** — 20,000 locations live, 12,500 companies participating; feedback indicated customers wanted more calendar control and job posting customization
- **Next Best Action** — new sales feature; team training held in Houston ahead of rollout

---

## October 2025 — Weekend Live Support
Launched live weekend customer support across phone and chat — a first for Homebase. Celebrated as a major milestone for the customer experience team.
