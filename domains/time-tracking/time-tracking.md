# Time Tracking

Homebase's time tracking product lets employees clock in and out, track breaks,
and gives managers visibility into labor hours. It's one of the two "core"
engagement features (alongside scheduling) — a location that uses time tracking
is more likely to be retained and to adopt other products.

---

## How It Works

Employees clock in/out via the Homebase mobile app, web dashboard, or a POS
timeclock device. Each clock-in creates a **timecard**. Managers can view, edit,
and approve timecards before running payroll.

Key capabilities:
- **Clock-in / clock-out** — manual or via mobile/POS
- **Break tracking** — configurable break types, can be set as mandatory for compliance
- **Overtime tracking** — alerts managers when employees approach overtime thresholds
- **Geofencing** — restricts clock-in to a physical location radius (paid plans only, Essentials+)
- **Manager edits** — managers can add, edit, or delete timecards on behalf of employees


## Key Metrics 

**% of Non-EE Edits** Percentage of edits that was done by either an OAM or one of the Payroll Assistants. Used to help assess whether we are saving OAMs time through reducing manual edits.

**Payroll Assistant MAU/WAU** Counts total locations that have used a Payroll Assistant within each month. Aggregate to calendar month of last 30 days.  

**# of Timecards Created** Counts number of Timecards that were created within a given time period.


## Payroll Assistants

Payroll Assistants are automation features that reduce the manual work managers
do around timecard cleanup before payroll. Available on tiers 3, 4 and
non-Clover locations.

### ACO (Assisted Clock Out)
Automatically generates a clock-out for employees who forgot. 
- GA: 2026-01-16

### ACI (Assisted Clock In)
Same concept as ACO but for clock-ins.

- Initial rollout: 2025-12-10
- GA: 2026-02-03

### Auto Payroll 
When payroll is run automatically

### Auto Clock Out
Automatically clock-outs for an employee who has already clocked in after a certain number of time. Enabled and configured in settings by OAM
