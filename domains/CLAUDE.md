# Domains

Product-level knowledge: what each domain does, how it works, and who uses it. For metric definitions and SQL, always follow the link to `data/`.

| Domain | Folder | What it covers |
|---|---|---|
| HRM (Team Management) | `hrm/` | Team scheduling, time tracking, employee management — core Homebase product |
| Time Tracking | `time-tracking/` | Clock-in/clock-out, timecards, overtime rules |
| Payroll | `payroll/` | Pay runs, wage calculation, tax filing |
| Hiring Assistant | `hiring-assistant/` | Job postings, applicant tracking, onboarding |
| Clover Embedded | `clover-embedded/` | Homebase timesheets inside Clover POS (Front Book + Back Book) |
| Cash Out | `cash-out/` | Early wage access product |
| **MSHR (Main Street Health Report)** | `mshr/` | Aggregated small business economy report — employment, wages, hiring, turnover |
