# Customer Segments

Load when answering questions about Homebase customer types, business verticals, or tier distribution.

## Primary Verticals

| Vertical | Examples | % of Base | Characteristics |
|---|---|---|---|
| Food & Dining | Restaurants, cafes, bars, food trucks | Largest segment | High turnover, complex scheduling, tip reporting |
| Retail | Shops, boutiques, convenience stores | Second largest | Seasonal fluctuations, part-time heavy |
| Services | Home services, salons, cleaning, auto repair | Growing segment | Mobile workforce, variable schedules |
| Healthcare | Clinics, dental offices, home health | Smaller segment | Compliance-heavy, credential tracking |

## SMB Archetypes

### The Solo Operator
- 1 location, 1-5 employees
- Owner does everything: scheduling, payroll, hiring
- Price-sensitive; often on Basic (free) tier
- Values simplicity over features
- HRM relevance: minimal onboarding needs, but when they hire, they need it to "just work"

### The Growing Business
- 1-2 locations, 10-25 employees
- Has a manager layer emerging; owner stepping back from daily operations
- Often on Essentials or Plus tier
- Starting to feel pain of manual HR processes
- HRM relevance: prime target for NHP and document management — currently doing it on paper or not at all

### The Multi-Location Operator
- 2-5 locations, 25-50+ employees
- Dedicated managers per location; owner is strategic
- Typically Plus or All-in-One tier
- Needs department-level permissions, compliance, and visibility across locations
- HRM relevance: heaviest HRM users — needs robust onboarding, document management, and employee records

## Tier Distribution

| Tier | Name | Approximate Distribution |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Basic (Free) | Majority of accounts — large free base |
| 2 | Essentials | Largest paid tier |
| 3 | Plus | Growing segment |
| 4 | All-in-One | Smallest but highest ARPU |

Payroll customers skew toward Plus and All-in-One tiers. Cash Out usage is independent of tier.

## User Roles Across Segments

| Role | What they do | Tech comfort | Primary device |
|---|---|---|---|
| Owner | Strategic decisions, billing, sometimes scheduling | Moderate | Web + Mobile |
| Admin | Day-to-day operations, often a trusted manager | Moderate-High | Web |
| Manager | Scheduling, timesheets, team communication | Moderate | Web + Mobile |
| Employee | Clock in/out, view schedule, complete onboarding | Variable (often lower) | Mobile-first |
